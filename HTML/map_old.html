The Map

This page presents Action Mapping Project data collected as part of a pilot study in 2016.

The participatory methods and analytical processes used by the Action Mapping Project were developed and refined over a two year pilot phase. The current Action Mapping Project project expands and improves on that foundational work in three ways:

  1. Formalizes existing informal partnerships between multiple agencies by establishing spaces where youth-led community mapping can be an ongoing effort.
  2. Adds an educational focus to the project by involving high school students and training them in the necessary technical / GIS skills to understand the data and produce results.
  3. Enhances UWT undergraduates' experiences by placing them into AMP Centers as mentors, teachers, and researchers.
